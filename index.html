Frequently Asked Questions

What is a standard drink?

A standard drink contains 10 grams of pure alcohol. For example: a 330ml beer at 5%, a 150ml glass of wine at 12%, or a 45ml shot of spirits at 40%.

What are WHO guidelines?

The World Health Organization recommends no more than 14 standard drinks per week, with at least 2 alcohol-free days per week. SoberStride Strides your consumption against these guidelines.

Can I set my own limits?

Yes! Go to Settings > Customization > Custom Guideline to set a personal monthly drink limit with your own name and target.

How do streaks work?

Your sober streak counts consecutive days without logging any drinks. Each day you stay sober increases your streak. Logging a drink resets it to zero.

How do I back up my data?

Go to Settings > Cloud Backup. On iOS, your data is backed up to iCloud. On Android, it's backed up to Google Drive.

Is my data private?

Absolutely. All your data is stored locally on your device. Cloud backups are stored in your personal iCloud or Google Drive account. We do not have access to any of your data. See our Privacy Policy for details.

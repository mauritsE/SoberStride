Introduction

SoberStride ("the App") is a personal drink tracking application. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what data the App collects, how it is stored, and your rights regarding that data.

Data We Collect

The App collects only the data you provide directly:

  • Drink logs - drink type, volume, alcohol percentage, timestamp, and optional notes
  • App preferences - theme settings, notification preferences, and custom guidelines
  • Sobriety streaks and badges - calculated from your drink logs

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using an on-device database. We do not operate any servers and do not have access to your data.

Cloud Backup

If you choose to enable cloud backup:

  • iOS: Data is backed up to your personal iCloud account using iCloud storage.
  • Android: Data is backed up to your personal Google Drive account.

Cloud backups are stored in your own account and are subject to Apple's or Google's respective privacy policies. We do not have access to your cloud backups.

Health Data

If you choose to export data to Apple Health or Google Fit, the App will request permission to write nutrition data. The App does not read any health data from these services. Health data sharing is entirely optional and controlled by you.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • We do not collect location data
  • We do not use analytics or tracking services
  • We do not display advertisements
  • We do not share any data with third parties

Notifications

The App may send local push notifications (daily reminders, badge achievements) if you enable them. These notifications are generated entirely on your device and are not sent through any external service.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by going to Settings > Reset Database in the App. You can also uninstall the App, which will remove all locally stored data. Cloud backups can be deleted through your iCloud or Google Drive settings.

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by anyone under the legal drinking age in their jurisdiction. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
